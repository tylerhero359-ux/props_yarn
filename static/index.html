User Guide

🗺️ Section Overview

⌂
Overview — Dashboard snapshot. Shows today's schedule, quick-access favorites, and recent tracker bets at a glance.
🗓️
Today's Games — Full slate with team injury notes and game status. Use this to spot missing key players before analyzing props.
📊
Player Analyzer — Deep-dive on one player prop. Pick a player, stat, and line to get hit rate, averages, trends, matchup data, and a confidence score.
🔍
Bet Finder — Bulk analysis mode. Enter multiple props (or import from Odds API) and the scanner ranks them by EV, edge, and hit rate.
📡
Live Tracker — Real-time monitoring for props you are tracking today. Add props here to watch their live stat as games progress.
🔗
Parlay Builder — Provide Odds API keys and event IDs; the tool scrapes props, analyzes all of them, and builds the statistically strongest N-leg parlay.
📈
Backtest Performance — Log your predictions before games, then resolve them after. Tracks win rate, ROI, and accuracy by confidence tier.

🧮 Key Metrics Explained

Hit Rate% of last N games where the player went OVER the line. Above 60% is strong; above 70% is elite.
AverageMean stat value across the sample window. Compare to the line — a big gap is your edge.
EdgeHow far the average sits from the line, as a percentage. Higher edge = more statistical buffer.
EV (Expected Value)Estimated profit per $100 based on hit rate × odds. Positive EV (green) = theoretically profitable long-term.
Confidence Score0–100 composite score built from hit rate, sample size, H2H data, and minutes trend. 70+ = High, 50–69 = Medium, below 50 = Low.
Confidence TierHigh = strong lean, large sample. Medium = moderate lean. Low = small sample or mixed signals.
H2H Hit RateHit rate in games specifically against today's opponent. Useful to confirm or override the overall trend.
vs. Position DeltaHow much opponents at the same position allow compared to league average. Positive = the defense is weak at defending this stat.
Back-to-BackWhether the player is playing on zero rest today. B2B typically reduces minutes and output — factor this in for OVER bets.
Minutes TrendWhether the player's minutes are trending UP, DOWN, or FLAT over recent games. Rising minutes usually means rising prop output.
Impact CountNumber of key teammates missing (injured/out). Higher impact count = player may absorb extra usage and volume.
Odds API CreditsEach "Load Events" or "Import Props" call costs credits. Use Check Balance (free) to see remaining credits without spending any.

🚀 Recommended Workflow

  1. Check Today's Games — Go to Today's Games to see the full slate. Note any injury news that could boost a player's usage or minutes.
  2. Fill the Key Vault — Go to Key Vault, paste your Odds API keys, then use Check credits so the app can rotate only usable keys.
  3. Load Events & Import Props — Click Load Events, pick a game from the dropdown, then click Import Props & Scan. The scanner will rank all available props by EV and edge.
  4. Deep-dive with Player Analyzer — Click any player row in the scanner to open Player Analyzer. Review hit rate, average, H2H, matchup, and environment signals. Use Analyzer Filters to narrow by Home/Away, win/loss context, or opponent quality.
  5. Send results to other tools — Use the three action buttons at the top of the Analyzer results:
    • Add to Slip — Adds the prop to your Prop Slip for a quick multi-pick summary.
    • 📡 Send to Tracker — Adds to Live Tracker so you can monitor the stat in real time during the game.
    • 📊 Send to Backtest — Pre-fills the Backtest log form so you can record this prediction before the game starts.
  6. Log predictions in Backtest — Before games tip off, use Backtest Performance to log your picks. After the game, enter the actual stat value to resolve each prediction as a hit or miss.
  7. Review your performance — The Backtest dashboard shows overall win rate, ROI, and accuracy broken down by confidence tier. Use this to learn which tier and stat types you call best.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Always log predictions in Backtest before the game starts — resolving after the fact defeats the purpose of tracking your forecast accuracy.
  • A High confidence tier alone is not enough. Check H2H hit rate against today's opponent and confirm no back-to-back or key teammate is missing.
  • Use the "Last N" filter (5/10/15/20 games) in the Analyzer. A player with a 70% hit rate over 20 games but 90% in the last 5 is on a hot streak — lean on the recent form.
  • In Bet Finder, sort by EV first to find the most mispriced props. Sort by hit rate to find the most consistent ones. The best bets usually score well on both.
  • Use Parlay Builder only when you have 2–3 High-tier props already identified. The builder will round out the remaining legs with the best available stats.
  • Save your Odds API key once and it will be remembered between sessions. You won't need to re-enter it.
  • The ✕ button on Backtest logs removes just that one entry. The Clear All button wipes the entire log — use it carefully.
