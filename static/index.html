How to fill it

One prop per line.

  • Imported Odds API rows are converted to decimal before scanning.
  • Use stat codes like PTS, REB, AST, 3PM, PRA.
  • Teams and opponents are auto-detected from the player.
  • The scanner ranks by EV first, then edge, then recent hit rate.
